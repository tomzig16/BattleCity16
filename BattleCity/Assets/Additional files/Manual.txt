Manual for Battle City remake (by Tomas Zigmantavièius)

This project was made for job application (Unity).

Player tank controls:
W (or arrow up) - Moves player up
A (or arrow left) - Moves player left
S (or arrow down) - Moves player down
D (or arrow right) - Moves player right
Space (or Num0) - Shots
Escape - Pauses game
